Welcome to Dragon Ball Z: Dokkan Battle - Otherworld

Dragon Ball Z: Dokkan Battle - Otherworld is a wiki dedicated to custom content of the hit mobile game Dragon Ball Z: Dokkan Battle, originally developed by Akatsuki.

Dragon Ball Z: Dokkan Battle, often shortened to simply Dokkan, a free-to-play mobile game based on the Dragon Ball anime franchise.

Our goal is to create a comprehensive encyclopedia for all official material related to this ongoing series. Edits from new editors are reviewed first, but any help we can get is greatly appreciated!
